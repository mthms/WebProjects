@This is a web-based library management system with the below features:
- Integrated electroic reservation system to allow users to reserve the books that they want to buy from the library.
- Integrated accounts balance to allow the users to pay cash or credit card and update their balance through the accountant of the library.
- Smart admin tool with all of the available functions to control the functionalities and authorizations.
- Easy resreve and receive process to allow users search, reserve and schedule the receive time in just 3 clicks.
